ASEAN’s Demographic Transition

Population Structure
25% - Age 60+
50% - Working Age
25% - Children
Population ageing • declining fertility • long-term care needs

ASEAN is shifting rapidly toward ageing societies, with people aged 60+ expected to reach one in four by 2050. Falling fertility and rising life expectancy reshape population structures, increasing demands for eldercare, social protection and healthcare. Some Member States may still benefit from remaining youth populations, but doing so requires investment in skills and employment opportunities.
