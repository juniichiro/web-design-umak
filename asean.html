About ASEAN Knowledge Hub

Driving ASEAN's digital future through research, collaboration, and human-centric innovation

Our Mission & Vision

The ASEAN Knowledge Hub serves as a central platform for knowledge sharing, research, and collaboration focused on ASEAN's digital transformation and human development.

Our Mission

To foster a resilient, inclusive digital future for ASEAN by providing evidence-based insights, facilitating regional cooperation, and promoting human-centric technological advancement.

Our Vision

An ASEAN community where digital transformation empowers all citizens, strengthens regional resilience, and creates sustainable prosperity through human-centric innovation.

ASEAN's Demographic Transition

Population ageing • Declining fertility • Long-term care needs

ASEAN is shifting rapidly toward ageing societies, with people aged 60+ expected to reach one in four by 2050. Falling fertility and rising life expectancy reshape population structures, increasing demands for eldercare, social protection and healthcare.

Skills for Future Work

Digital jobs • AI roles • Upskilling & Reskilling

Technology and automation are reshaping jobs in ASEAN. AI, data science, robotics and fintech roles are expanding, while routine clerical jobs decline. Nearly half of workers will need new skills within five years, emphasizing lifelong learning and adaptable training systems.

Education Trends & Challenges

Learning recovery • STEM • Lifelong learning • SDG4

ASEAN education systems face pressures from digital shifts, demographic change and pandemic disruptions. Many Member States invest below global benchmarks, highlighting the need for stronger financing, teacher development and digital integration.
